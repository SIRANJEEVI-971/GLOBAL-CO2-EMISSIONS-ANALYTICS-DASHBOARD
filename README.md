# 🌍 Global CO₂ Emissions Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Global CO₂ Emissions Analytics Dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI.

The dashboard analyzes worldwide CO₂ emissions by country, region, population, income groups, and emission growth trends. It transforms raw environmental data into interactive business intelligence reports that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze global CO₂ emissions.
- Compare emissions across regions.
- Study emission trends over time.
- Analyze emissions by income group.
- Compare country-wise emissions.
- Monitor yearly emission growth.
- Support sustainability reporting.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Total CO₂ Emissions KPI
- Total Population KPI
- Total Countries KPI
- Average CO₂ Emissions KPI
- Last Year CO₂ Emissions KPI
- Region-wise CO₂ Analysis
- Country-wise Emissions
- Income Group Analysis
- Emission Trend Analysis
- Interactive Filters

## 📈 Key Insights

- East Asia & Pacific contribute the highest CO₂ emissions.
- Emissions have increased significantly over the past decades.
- High-income countries contribute a major share of emissions.
- Large population countries dominate total emissions.
- Regional differences highlight environmental inequality.
